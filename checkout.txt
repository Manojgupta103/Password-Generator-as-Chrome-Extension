Step 1 : clone this repo in your system

Step 2 : to open the project as chrome extension

	Step 1 : type -> chrome://extensions in your chrome address bar

	Step 2 : toggle developer mode on top right corner

	Step 3 : click Load unpacked on top left corner

	Step 4 : select AttMan/AttMan and open this folder

	Step 5 : chrome extension is added to your browser now start developing

All the basic requirements are met, now start developing.
You can choose any ide of your choice to develop but i would recommend vscode.
